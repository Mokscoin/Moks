Welcome to MoksCoin

Moks' prototype is a Tibetan macaque. Fifteen years ago, on a stormy night, she went out foraging while pregnant but unfortunately stepped on a trap and was captured by a hunter. Although her wound was treated, she lost her left hand forever. Saved by kind people, she gradually recovered and survived to this day. Her plump and round appearance has made her a popular internet celebrity monkey. Moks' story tells of the miracle of rebirth from adversity and conveys the power of kindness and hope.
