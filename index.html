Moks is a Tibetan macaque. On a stormy night fifteen years ago, she went out to look for food while pregnant, but unfortunately stepped on a trap and was found and taken away by a hunter. Although the wound was simply treated, she lost her left hand forever. She was rescued by kind people, gradually recovered, got rid of the pain and survived to this day. Her chubby and round appearance made her a popular online celebrity monkey. Moks' story not only tells the miracle of rebirth from adversity, but also conveys the power of kindness and hope.
