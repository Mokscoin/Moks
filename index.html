Moks is a Tibetan macaque with a remarkable story. Fifteen years ago, on a stormy night, Moks, then pregnant, ventured out for food but stepped into a trap. Caught and taken by hunters, she lost her left hand despite receiving basic treatment. Saved by a kind soul, she was taken to a mountain temple, where she recovered from her past wounds and has lived ever since. Her round, chubby figure and playful spirit have made her a beloved internet icon. Moks' journey is not just about survival but also a symbol of kindness and hope.
